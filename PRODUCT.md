# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

ALPHA TECHNO SOFT serves Thai organizations that need practical help designing, installing, securing, and supporting business-critical IT infrastructure. The primary visitors are decision makers, IT managers, operations teams, and procurement stakeholders evaluating whether the company can be trusted with network, cyber security, cloud, endpoint, data protection, and workplace technology work.

## Product Purpose

This website presents ALPHA TECHNO SOFT CO., LTD. as a capable, responsive IT infrastructure and cyber security partner. Success means visitors quickly understand the service scope, see credible proof through certifications and technology partners, and feel confident enough to request a consultation.

## Brand Personality

Secure, expert, responsive. The voice should feel technically competent and calm, with enough energy to signal active protection and modern infrastructure readiness. Thai and English copy can coexist, but both should stay direct, business-focused, and easy to scan.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS-template cyber visuals, vague buzzword-heavy security copy, and overly dark hacker aesthetics that feel theatrical instead of trustworthy. Avoid design patterns that make the company look like a reseller catalog rather than an expert implementation and support partner.

## Design Principles

- Lead with operational trust: every page should make the company feel reliable, certified, and ready for real business environments.
- Show practical coverage: make the service range easy to understand without burying visitors in vendor jargon.
- Keep confidence calm: use strong cyber/security signals without making the interface feel alarmist or gimmicky.
- Make contact feel natural: consultation paths should be visible, direct, and low-friction.
- Respect bilingual context: Thai content should carry local clarity while English service labels preserve industry recognition.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA behavior: readable contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, skip navigation, meaningful alt text for informative imagery, responsive layouts from mobile through desktop, and reduced-motion alternatives for decorative or ambient animation.
